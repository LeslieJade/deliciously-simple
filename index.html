Perfectly Easy Blueberry Muffins

  • Servings: 9 Muffins
  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Source: Smitten Kitchen

This recipe makes just nine delicious blueberry muffins. But if you're feeding a crowd, just double the batch! There's no need to break out the standing mixer for this recipe, it all comes together by hand. Not only that, this recipe gives you options! You can choose to use fresh or frozen blueberries and opt for yogurt or sour cream. You can choose to give your muffins a crunchy top by sprinkling with raw sugar prior to baking, or simply skip it altogether. It's up to you! Also, this recipe is made for blueberry lovers and calls for a whopping 1 1/2 cups of fresh or frozen berries. If that seems a little much for your tastes, you may cut down the amount to 1 cup. So what are you waiting for? Let's make some muffins!

EQUIPMENT

  • Muffin tin
  • Paper liners or nonstick spray
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Smaller bowls, to hold ingredients
  • Microplane, or citrus zester
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Cooling rack

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Finely grated zest from 1/2 a lemon
  • 3/4 cup plain yogurt or sour cream
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher or sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups blueberries, fresh or frozen (no need to defrost)
  • 3 tablespoons raw (turbinado) sugar (optional)

BAKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Line muffin tin with 9 paper liners or spray each cup with nonstick spray.
  3. Pour butter in a large bowl and whisk in sugar, zest, yogurt or sour cream, and egg until smooth.
  4. Whisk in baking powder, baking soda and salt until fully combined.
  5. Lightly fold in the flour and blueberries.
  6. Divide batter among prepared muffin cups and if using, sprinkle each with 1 teaspoon raw sugar.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until the tops are golden brown.
  8. Remove from oven and allow to cool for a few minutes before moving muffins to cooling rack.
  9. Enjoy!
